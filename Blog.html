IMPACT Of The TECHNOLOGY
In My
DAILY LIFE

What is Technology?

Technology can be defined as the science or knowledge put into practice to solve problems or invent useful tools. It brings about a change to the human environment. It can also be called a development making human life easier, luxurious and sustainable. For exmaple : The prehistoric means to create a fire was to strike the stones together or to use the friction of the woods. But, today the technology has given us a flame thrower. The tiresome walking to travel a distance is replaced by vehicles, aeroplanes, ships and trains. The communication and interconnection between people has grately increased due to the invention and use of internet.

How has it affected peoples daily life?

Along with the advancement in time, there has been an advancement in the technology. Along with peoples knowledge the technology used in day to day life has improved. The traditional way of gringing the maslas is now made by grinders and mixtures. The simplest example can be the housing and clothing. The caves have been improved with time as huts, cottages, houses, buildings, and even mansions. The leaf used to cover the skin and to protect one from cold has turned into tech involved jackets with builtin heaters and earphones. The advancement in science and the achievements it gave has given us countless technologies. From the nip of the pen to the zip of the pen a technology is used.

From making the manual labour work easier to overtaking the work technology has changed the lives of the peoples. Now there is almost nothing a man can achieve. Can you imagine? the man that faught to survive with other men are now a part of the single world. From running in the fields to flying in the sky, from worshiping the moon to landing on the moon, form giving birth without even a painkiller to giving birth in the world's most convinient faculties without even feeling a single pain, how a persons life has changed.
The kid who used to play thief and police with friends in the park now plays call of duty warzone and PUBG online with the world. The things that technology has changed in peoples lifestyle is countless and form those countless things even less than 1% was mentioned above.

Impact of Technology In My Daily Life

As I am a student, the advancement in technology has affected my daily life in a huge way. It has changed the way I think, the way I feel, the way I read, the way I interact with people and many more. I remember the days playing football in the fields, now its just me and the boys playing fifa infront of a screen. The sweat, the fun and the relaxtation I felt back in the days has turned into cussing, frustration and boredom.
Even the way of eudcation has changed. I who hadn't even seen a computer in the childhood is now learning stuffs using one. The bulky notes I used to own has now changed to a simple word file or a pdf. The fee payment has also changed as the schools that didnt use cheques and made the cash compulsary now hardly does the card transaction. The ATM machine which I had only read about or seen in the movies, is being used by me. Most importantly due to the pandemic the same schools that had banned the use of mobiles and laptops is now encouraging the parents to buy one for their child. The traditional prison like education system has now been digitalised and more entertaining.
